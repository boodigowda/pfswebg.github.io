ABOUT

HISTORY  | TIMELINE  | LEADERSHIP

PFSweb, Inc., leads brands to commerce success. Across two key business units – LiveArea for professional services and PFS for order fulfillment services – we focus on smooth operations and intelligent technologies to drive conversions, empower brands, and deliver outstanding customer experiences. From creating unique, seamlessly designed flagship sites to picking, packing, and shipping orders, our teams are experts in all areas of digital commerce. Major brands and other companies turn to PFSweb to optimize customer experiences and enhance traditional and online business channels, commerce without compromise.

HISTORY

Cut from the cloth of an international wholesale distributor, PFSweb, Inc. was established in 1994 to provide customer care and logistics services to clients in the budding eCommerce industry. Over time, we developed alongside the industry, bringing in professional services to round out our operations offering. Today, this blend of services makes us the only platform-agnostic global commerce services company in the market.

FACT SHEET

TIMELINE

Michael Willoughby Named CEO

Previously the Vice President of eCommerce and President of PFSweb, Michael (Mike) Willoughby was named CEO in 2013. Mike’s extensive background in technology and unrivaled company tenure made him the perfect fit to lead PFSweb.

Brand Refresh

In September 2016, PFSweb began rolling out a brand refresh for our operations and professional services brands. Now, we are simply known as “PFS” and “LiveArea,” respectively, with new logos to match our savvy, forward-thinking offerings. We compete in multiple segments of commerce with a variety of solutions and services.

PFSweb, Inc. Founded

Born from a wholesale distributor of computer and office automation supplies, PFSweb, Inc. was founded by former CEO Mark Layton and was initially created to perform order processing, fulfillment, and call center services for retailers as the demand for outsourcing grew in the market.
